Meet the Kelburn Family

A local family business built on respect, compassion, and a genuine desire to help.

Our Story

Kelburn Property Clearance LTD is a true family business, started by husband and wife team Darren and Jo, together with their sons Jack and Tom. With deep roots in West Sussex, we’ve grown our business on the values of trust, reliability, and a personal touch.

Our family is proud to help other families, landlords, and local businesses through what can sometimes be emotional or difficult clearances. We understand the importance of respect and sensitivity, and we always go the extra mile to make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.

With a background in helping people—including experience in the ambulance service—our team brings calm, compassion, and professionalism to every job. We believe in doing things the right way: with honesty, hard work, and care for our community and the environment.

Whether you need a full house clearance or just a single room, you’ll always get the warm, personal service our family is known for.
