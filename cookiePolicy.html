Cookie Policy

Last updated: 31 October 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Kelburn Property Clearance ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website kelburnpropertyclearance.co.uk.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide a better user experience, remember your preferences, and analyse how our website is used.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. For example, we may use Google Analytics or similar tools to collect anonymous data such as page views and time spent on the site.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember choices you make (such as your language or region) and provide enhanced features.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure our website works correctly
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how our website is used and improve it

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies, delete existing cookies, or alert you when websites set cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how our website functions.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services that appear on our pages, such as embedded maps or analytics providers. We do not control these cookies. For more information, please review the privacy or cookie policies of these third parties.

Updates to This Policy
