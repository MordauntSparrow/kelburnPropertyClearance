Our Story

Kelburn Property Clearance Ltd was founded by Jo and Darren, who shared a simple idea — to build a family-run business that makes a real difference for people during life’s transitions.

Together, they combined their experience, community values, and care for others to create a service built on trust, reliability, and compassion. From the very beginning, Jo’s sons, Jack and Tom, and Darren’s sons, Tyler and Caytlin, have supported the business and continue to play an important part in helping it grow.

As a family, we’re proud to support other families, landlords, and local businesses through what can sometimes be emotional or challenging clearances. We understand the importance of sensitivity and respect, and we always go the extra mile to make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.

With backgrounds in care, community, and emergency services, our team brings calm professionalism and genuine empathy to every job. We believe in doing things the right way — with honesty, hard work, and care for people and the environment.

Whether it’s a full property clearance or just a single room, you’ll always receive the personal, thoughtful service that defines the Kelburn family.
