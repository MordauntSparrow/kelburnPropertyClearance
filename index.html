Our Story

Kelburn Property Clearance Ltd was founded by Jo and Darren with a simple idea: to build a family-run property clearance business that makes a real difference during life's transitions.

By combining their experience, community values, and genuine care for others, they created a trusted house clearance and probate clearance service built on reliability, respect, and compassion. From the beginning, Jo's sons Jack and Tom, and Darren's children Tyler and Caytlin, have supported the business and continue to play an important role in helping it grow.

As a family-run company, we're proud to support families, landlords, and local businesses across Sussex and Hampshire through emotional and challenging clearances. We provide sensitive and professional house clearances, including probate clearances, with care and understanding at every stage.

With backgrounds in care, community, and emergency services, our team brings calm professionalism and genuine empathy to every property clearance we carry out. We believe in doing things the right way with honesty, hard work, and respect for people and the environment.

Whether you need a full property clearance, a probate clearance, or help with a single room, you'll receive the personal and thoughtful service that defines the Kelburn family across Sussex and Hampshire.
